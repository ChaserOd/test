Introducing Pancono on Testnet on Ethereum Sepolia Network

Alongside launching Pancono on its own native blockchain, we are also bringing Pancono to the Ethereum blockchain. To prepare for this, we have opened a testnet on the Sepolia ETH network where users can join, complete simple tasks, and later receive Pancono tokens directly in their Ethereum Mainnet wallets.
